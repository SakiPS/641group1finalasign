About Suzanne Tulenko

Suzanne Tulenko graduated dental school from The Medical University of South Carolina in May of 1990. She then completed a post graduate residency at Loma Linda V.A. Hospital.

She worked for a couple of years as an associate and then purchased her own practice from the late great Dr. William Ashfield.

Our New Office

Dr. Tulenko rented space for 15 years until she was finally able to build her own dental practice in the heart of old Chula Vista. The doors opened up to our new state of the art dental practice December 2005.
